Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the YouTube Thumbnail Preview & Safe Zone Tester handles information when you use the site. The tool is designed to be simple and lightweight. A core part of that design is local image handling. When you upload a thumbnail image into the preview tool, the file is processed inside your browser so the page can render desktop, mobile, and sidebar previews without requiring a server-side upload step.

In normal use, your thumbnail image is not stored by this site, added to a user account, or saved to a database. The image stays on your device and is used only to generate the in-browser preview and export output that you request. If you close the page or refresh the session, that local image state is cleared unless your browser itself restores the page state. This local approach is intended to reduce unnecessary data collection and make the tool faster to use.

Like most websites, the site may still receive basic technical information through the hosting environment. That can include standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, referring page, device information, timestamps, and requested files. This kind of information is generally used for security, performance monitoring, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention. It is not used by the thumbnail tool itself to recreate or store your uploaded image.

Cookies or similar technologies may be used for essential site behavior, traffic measurement, or future improvements. If privacy-friendly analytics are added later, they may collect high-level usage information such as page views, popular content, and device categories. That information would be used to understand which guides are most helpful and which screen sizes matter most for design decisions. If third-party advertising is enabled in the future, those ad providers may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies and consent requirements.

This site may link to other pages on the same domain as well as general resources related to YouTube thumbnail design. Once you leave this site or interact with third-party tools or ad services, their privacy practices apply instead of this policy. You should review the relevant policies for any external service you use.

If you contact the site directly, any information you include in your message such as your name, email address, or the details of your question will be used only to respond to you or follow up on the issue you reported. Contact messages are not sold and are not used to build a marketing profile.

This policy may be updated from time to time if the site adds analytics, changes hosting, or introduces advertising features. The current version will always be posted on this page. If you have a privacy question, you can write to feeligfeelig@gmail.com.
