Nevada

Nevada annual fee calculator

Nevada entities typically deal with two recurring state charges at the annual filing stage: the annual list fee and the state business license renewal fee. This calculator combines the published amounts for LLCs and corporations and shows the basic late penalties published in the statutes used for this page.

Calculator scope

What the Nevada calculator includes and what it leaves out

What the calculator includes

The calculator adds the annual list fee and the state business license renewal fee. It also shows the published late penalties used for overdue annual lists and overdue state business license renewal payments.

What the calculator does not include

It does not include registered-agent fees, local business licenses, expedited service, amendments, reinstatement costs, or any other private-service costs. This page is only tracking the recurring state-level items shown in the source set below.

Statutory basis

Rule Published amount or timing
