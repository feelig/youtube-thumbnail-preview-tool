Nevada

Nevada annual fee calculator

Nevada entities typically deal with two recurring state charges at the annual filing stage: the annual list fee and the state business license renewal fee. This calculator combines the published amounts for LLCs and corporations and shows the basic late penalties published in the statutes used for this page.

Last reviewed: March 11, 2026 Source: Nevada Revised Statutes

At a glance

Annual list deadline Last day of the month of the entity's anniversary date.
LLC recurring state total $350 before late penalties.
Corporation recurring state total $650 before late penalties.

Interactive breakdown

Nevada LLC recurring annual fees

Annual list fee $150
State business license renewal $200
Recurring state total $350
Total if filed late $525

Uses the Nevada LLC annual list fee in NRS 86.263 and the general state business license renewal fee in NRS 76.130.

What the calculator includes

The calculator adds the annual list fee and the state business license renewal fee. It also shows the published late penalties used for overdue annual lists and overdue state business license renewal payments.

What the calculator does not include

It does not include registered-agent fees, local business licenses, expedited service, amendments, reinstatement costs, or any other private-service costs. This page is only tracking the recurring state-level items shown in the source set below.

Statutory basis

Source rules behind the Nevada totals

Rule Published amount or timing
Nevada LLC annual list fee $150 with the annual list due by the last day of the anniversary month
Nevada corporation annual list fee $150 with the annual list due by the last day of the anniversary month
State business license renewal fee $200 generally, or $500 for corporations organized under chapters 78, 78A, or 78B and foreign corporations under chapter 80
Late penalties shown here $75 annual-list penalty and $100 state-business-license late penalty
