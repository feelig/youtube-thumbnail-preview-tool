North Carolina

North Carolina annual report deadline for LLCs and corporations

Use this page if you need the North Carolina annual report deadline or filing fee. The first split to make is entity type: LLCs and L3Cs usually use April 15, while corporations use a fiscal-year deadline.

Recently checked Checked: March 17, 2026 Reviewed by FinLogic Hub Editorial Team 5 official sources Source: NC Secretary of State

At a glance

LLC or L3C deadline April 15 each year after the year of creation.
LLC fee $200 paper, $202 by ACH, or $203 by credit card online.
Corporation fee $25 paper, $20 by ACH, or $21 by credit card online.

What to know first

What to check before you file in North Carolina

Entity type Published due date Online fee Paper fee
Business corporation 15th day of the fourth month following fiscal year end $20 ACH or $21 credit card $25
Limited Liability Company (LLC or L3C) April 15 each year after the year of creation $202 ACH or $203 credit card $200
