Georgia annual registration deadline

Georgia uses annual registration instead of a traditional annual report label for many entities. The current Secretary of State guidance used for this page points to an April 1 deadline and a $50 filing fee for the entity types covered by the filing portal.

Last reviewed: March 11, 2026 Based on Georgia SOS guidance and filing portal

At a glance

Deadline April 1 each year.
Published filing fee $50 for the covered annual registration filing.
Best next step Use the One Click Annual Registration portal and confirm the entity shown there.

This page stays conservative on late-fee language because the Georgia portal and guidance used for this review did not provide a flat late-fee number in a source we could preserve directly today.

What this page is designed to answer

If you landed here from an old Georgia annual-report URL, the practical question is usually the same: what is the deadline, what is the filing fee, and where do I go to file? This page answers those points first and avoids inventing penalty math that is not clearly published in the current review set.

What to do if you are already late

Go directly to the Georgia filing portal and review the current instructions for your entity before relying on a third-party late-fee estimate. If the Secretary of State later publishes a clearer flat late-fee or reinstatement schedule, this page should be updated to match it.
