Pennsylvania annual report fee and deadline

Use this page if you need the Pennsylvania annual report window or the standard $7 filing fee. Start with the entity type, because the filing window changes across corporations, LLCs, and other associations.

At a glance

LLC filing window January 1 through September 30.

Pennsylvania LLC Use the January 1 to September 30 window

If the record is an LLC, start with the long January through September filing window and then confirm the filing in the state portal.

Corporation or association Do not assume the LLC window applies

Pennsylvania shifts the filing window by association type, so corporations and other associations should verify the matching month range first.

Fee and late timing The standard fee is $7, but enforcement starts later

The state lists a $7 standard filing fee, while administrative action begins with reports due in 2027 rather than the first filing year.

What to know first

What to check before you file in Pennsylvania

Association type Filing dates Published fee
Corporations, business and nonprofit, domestic and foreign January 1 to June 30 $7 for business corporations, $0 for nonprofits
Limited liability companies, domestic and foreign January 1 to September 30 $7, or $0 for LLCs with a not-for-profit purpose
Limited partnerships, LLPs, business trusts, professional associations January 1 to December 31 $7, or $0 for qualifying not-for-profit entities
