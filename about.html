About FinLogic Hub

FinLogic Hub summarizes selected state filing deadlines, recurring fees, and official filing paths in a shorter format. The goal is simple: help you find the right state rule and the right official source faster.

Every live guide starts from an official state source and shows a visible review date. These pages are for planning only. If the state record or filing portal says something different, follow the official source.

Editorial rules for live pages

  • Use official state sources for deadlines, filing fees, and late-payment language.
  • Keep entity types separate when the state applies different rules.
  • Show the review date on the page.
  • Link back to the official filing page or statute.
  • Say "not clearly stated" when the official guidance does not publish a number.
  • Remove unsupported or outdated pages instead of keeping uncertain information live.
